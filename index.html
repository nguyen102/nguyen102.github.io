Events in DC

An events aggregator for the Washington, DC region.

New

I'm the type of person who loves adventure and exploration, but with a busy schedule, it's hard to make the time to figure out what cool events are going on in town. In the past I use to rely on searching FB events and general Googling. That was until I decided to build "Events in DC". "Events in DC" is an events aggregator that searches local DC websites for new and interesting events going on in town. It does this once a day and presents its findings in a single webpage. In conjunction with that, it also gives users the option of having a daily email sent to their inbox.

Fleye Ball

The Fleye Ball was a gadget that my two friends and I built for our final project during Junior year of college. For the final project our task was to design and come up with a project that can be built, tested, and presented to the class. Our team decided to build the Fleye Ball which was designed to take aerial photos of the terrain. When a user wants to see his surroundings from a bird's eye view, he would throw the Fleye Ball in the air by using the ball's tail to gain speed and momentum. The Fleye Ball would detect that it reached the apex of its flight through the use of an accelerometer. At that point it would turn downward and capture a picture of the landscape. Once the ball comes hurling down and is caught by the user, it would then wirelessly transmit the picture of the landscape to the user's computer. To see a YouTube video of the Fleye Ball in action click "Find out more".
